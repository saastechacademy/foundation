## Every individual should have a good understanding of the following areas:

- OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities


- Understanding different types of vulnerabilities, how to reproduce them, and how to determine their severity and classification


- Setup and usage of various tools such as Nmap, OWASP ZAP Proxy, and paid tools like Burp Suite or Acunetix


- Strong knowledge of VAPT (Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing)


- Understanding the process of reporting vulnerabilities in Apache OFBiz and the associated procedures

  https://ofbiz.apache.org/security.html 
  
  https://www.apache.org/security/
  
  https://github.com/apache/ofbiz-framework/blob/trunk/SECURITY.md


- Analysis of the last 20 vulnerabilities in Apache OFBiz, including reproducing them, reviewing the fixes, and understanding them in detail


- In-depth knowledge of Apache OFBiz framework internals such as login, logout, session management, security permissions, service engine, entity engine, and Tomcat server configuration (separate documents will be prepared for these topics)


- Good understanding of various business processes in Apache OFBiz(separate documents will be prepared for these topics)

- Setup of Antigravity tool - Expert in using this tool
